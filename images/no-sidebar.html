About us

Seeing as you're trusting us with your work and grades, it's understandable you want to know more about the team.

PROOFiT developed unofficially in 2016 in London as a affordable service to aid the many international students who found struggles in formally writing and submitting essays or other assignments in English. The service was first aimed at those of whom english is not their first language, in order to provide them a support tool when submitting their assignments. Now PROOFiT has expanded to Singapore, and now caters not only tasks in English but also basic and intermediate levels of Mandarin. Our customer base is no longer plainly those who grew up in a foreign country with a foreign dialect, but simply to offer a affordable and fast peace of mind service. We aim to pick up on the silly mistakes that even the smartest of us make - To get you the best grade possible
